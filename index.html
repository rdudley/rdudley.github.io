ABOUT

I'm Randolph Dudley, a developer living in Athens, Georgia and currently working for Planet Studio, LLC in Atlanta, Georgia. With over 12 years of experience, I've developed a strong passion for building tools, creating rewarding user interfaces, solving problems, and helping push concepts or ideas into reality. I've been involved in a wide variety of projects, adapting my role as needed to see a project from start to finish. I currently develop with a standard toolkit of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP, and I'm always ready to learn something new.

 

Over the years I've had the pleasure to work with some really great clients including PBS, Coca-Cola, ING, Scripps Networks Interactive, The Weather Channel, WeTV, Perfetti van Melle, IHG, Discovery, A&E, R.E.M., Paul Simon and Widespread Panic.

 

Outside of the office I enjoy being in the great outdoors, taking insanely long road trips, photography, foraging, and cooking. I cofounded The Four Coursemen in Athens, a (not so secret) underground dinner group that has been featured on The Cooking Channel, GPTV, and in many publications including Southern Living, Garden & Gun, and Food & Wine.
