Perfetti van Melle

The Perfetti van Melle Sales Resource Center has been a great project to work on, and a valuable resource for our client. It features a full compliment of content management tools, a document library of over 5,000 files, user tracking and analytics, and the ability to create PDF product sheets using data exported directly out of SAP.

 

I've worked on many other projects for Perfetti including several sweepstakes sites, a live Twitter feed web application for conferences, a custom blog for their VP of Sales, and a website focused on collaborative behavior with a rating and voting system.
